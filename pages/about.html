Meet Jo Smith

Capturing the Essence of Nature & Life Through the Lens

My Journey Into Photography

Born and raised amidst the stunning landscapes of Dundee, Scotland, my passion for photography blossomed from an early age. I've always found solace and inspiration in the natural world around me, from the serene lochs to the dramatic coastlines. This deep connection to nature has shaped my artistic vision, leading me to specialise in capturing its raw beauty and intricate details.

My academic journey took me through university right here in Dundee, where I honed my technical skills and developed a unique photographic style. While nature photography remains my true calling, my diverse experiences have allowed me to excel in various genres, from intimate portraits to vibrant event coverage, always with a commitment to professionalism and artistic integrity.
